The Firm

A Firm Built on
Integrity & Results

O.M. Otieno & Company Advocates is a professional law firm based at Shabana Building, Hospital Road, Kisii — conveniently situated at the Safaricom Care Centre complex in the heart of the city.

Led by Ouma Maurice Otieno, the firm has established an active High Court litigation practice with matters argued at Kisii, Kisumu, Siaya, and Bomet courts, as well as the commercial and tax divisions. The firm represents individuals, businesses, and institutions across a comprehensive range of legal disciplines.

The firm operates six days a week — including Saturdays — ensuring that quality legal counsel remains accessible to clients whenever it is needed. Appointments are available by walk-in or prior arrangement, and consultations via phone and WhatsApp are offered for clients across the region.
